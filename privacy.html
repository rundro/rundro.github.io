Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 27, 2024

Introduction

At Rundro Apps, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we handle any information collected through our mobile applications.

Information We Collect

Our apps are designed to work offline and collect minimal data:

  • Game progress and settings (stored locally on your device)
  • Basic usage analytics (if you agree to share analytics data)
  • Device information required for app functionality

How We Use Your Information

Any information collected is used solely for:

  • Improving app performance and user experience
  • Fixing bugs and technical issues
  • Developing new features based on user behavior

Data Storage

Most data is stored locally on your device. We do not maintain user accounts or store personal information on our servers.

Third-Party Services

Our apps may use third-party services for:

  • Analytics (Apple App Analytics)
  • Crash reporting
  • App Store functionality

Children's Privacy

Our apps do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent and discover that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at support@pocketcapital.com.
