Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 27, 2024

Introduction

We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we handle any information collected through our mobile applications.

Information We Collect

Our apps collect minimal data directly:

  • Game progress and settings (stored locally on your device)
  • Basic usage analytics (if you agree to share analytics data)
  • Device information required for app functionality

Third-Party Advertising

Our apps may include third-party advertising. These advertisers, not us, may collect certain data as outlined below. This data collection is controlled by you through Apple's App Tracking Transparency (ATT) framework, which allows you to choose whether advertisers can track your activity across apps and websites.

Data Used to Track You

The following data may be used by advertisers to track you across apps and websites owned by other companies:

  • Location
  • Identifiers
  • Usage Data
  • Diagnostics

Data Linked to You

The following data may be collected by advertisers and linked to your identity:

  • Location
  • Identifiers
  • Usage Data
  • Diagnostics

Data Not Linked to You

The following data may be collected but is not linked to your identity:

  • Diagnostics

App Tracking Transparency

When you first launch our app, you will be prompted to allow or deny tracking through Apple's App Tracking Transparency feature. You can change your preference at any time in your device's Settings app under Privacy > Tracking.

If you choose to opt out of tracking, advertisers will have limited ability to collect and use the data described above. However, you may still see advertisements, but they may be less relevant to your interests.

How We Use Your Information

Any information collected directly by us is used solely for:

  • Improving app performance and user experience
  • Fixing bugs and technical issues
  • Developing new features based on user behavior

Data Storage

Most data is stored locally on your device. We do not maintain user accounts or store personal information on our servers.

Third-Party Services

Our apps may use third-party services for:

  • Analytics (Apple App Analytics)
  • Advertising networks
  • Crash reporting
  • App Store functionality

Each third-party service provider has its own privacy policy governing how they handle your data. We encourage you to review these policies.

Your Rights

You have the right to control how your data is used. You can:

  • Opt out of tracking via App Tracking Transparency
  • Reset your advertising identifier in your device settings
  • Request deletion of any data we directly control by contacting us

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at Contact Us.
